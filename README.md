    [1.0] O M N I C H A N N E L   O N B O A R D I N G   ( T U T O R I A L )
    ===============================================================================
    
    You begin the game in the 'Incubator' zone. Your first objective is to acquire 
    your core competencies. Head north to the glowing terminal and press 'Action' 
    to ingest the API payload.
    
    * PRO-TIP: Don't waste your skill points on 'Waterfall Methodology' early on. 
      It has a massive cast time and heavily debuffs your time-to-market. Instead, 
      spec completely into 'Agile Sprints' to maximize your iterative velocity.
    
    Grab the "Docker Container" from the chest on the left. This acts as your 
    inventory for the rest of the game, keeping your dependencies completely 
    isolated and highly scalable.
    
    ===============================================================================
    [2.0] L E V E L   1 :   N A V I G A T I N G   T H E   D A T A   L A K E
    ===============================================================================
    
    The Data Lake is a massive, unstructured zone. It is very easy to get lost 
    here and drown in redundant analytics.
    
    1. As soon as you enter, cast 'Data Governance' to reveal the hidden pathways.
    2. Avoid the 'Dark Data' mobs. They have high armor and drop zero actionable 
       insights. If you pull aggro, use 'Predictive Algorithms' to dodge their 
       attacks.
    3. You'll find a locked gate requiring a 'Zero-Trust Architecture' key. To 
       get this, you need to complete the side-quest for the CISO NPC located in 
       the eastern quadrant. He will ask you to fetch 5 'Multi-Factor Auth' tokens.
    4. Once unlocked, seamlessly integrate into the next zone.

    [3.0] B O S S   G U I D E :   T H E   L E G A C Y   M O N O L I T H

          .---------------.
         /               /|    BOSS: Legacy Monolith
        /_______________/ |    HP: 9,999,999 (Technical Debt)
        |  ___ ___ ___  | |    WEAKNESS: Microservices
        | |   |   |   | | |    IMMUNITY: Innovation
        | |___|___|___| | /
        |_______________|/
    
    This is the first major bottleneck of the game. The Legacy Monolith takes up 
    the entire server room and attacks by dropping massive downtime events on 
    your party. 
    
    Phase 1:
    Do NOT attack it head-on. Its 'Spaghetti Code' armor will deflect any direct 
    feature requests. Instead, you need to chip away at it using the 'Strangler 
    Fig Pattern'. 
    
    Phase 2:
    Once its HP drops below 50%, it will cast 'Vendor Lock-in'. Quickly counter 
    this by deploying an 'Open-Source Mesh Network'. You must aggressively 
    decouple its core databases while spamming 'Continuous Integration / 
    Continuous Deployment (CI/CD)'. 
    
    Phase 3:
    When the Monolith starts glowing red, it is about to crash. Use 'Auto-Scaling 
    Groups' to absorb the impact. Once the smoke clears, the Monolith will shatter 
    into thousands of easily manageable, scalable microservices. Loot the 'Golden 
    Uptime SLA' it drops.
